About Makorokocho Hotel

Where Comfort Meets Modern Living in Dar es Salaam

Our Story

Makorokocho Hotel was established in 2025 with the aim to provide a restful, comfortable, and modern stay for both tourists and locals in Dar es Salaam. Our hotel offers a blend of traditional Tanzanian hospitality alongside contemporary accommodations — perfect for your holiday, business trip, or weekend getaway.
